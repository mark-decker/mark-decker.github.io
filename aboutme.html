About myself

Ever since I was a little kid I've always had a passion for finding out how things work. When I was little I took apart the remote control and was unable to put it back together. I got in trouble. Thankfully my parents bought me a bread board, a chemistry, and let me build my own go kart with a lawn mower engine in elementary school. That kept me out of trouble and kept my desire to discover alive. My passion for science and research is a simple extension of this curiosity. I want to know how physical world around us works.

Although I am an American Citizen I currently live and work in Sydney Australia, and have done so the past six years. I have an amazing partner and a beautiful daughter. Living and working Sydney has been a great opportunity to work with world class researchers and experience a new culture. My future will hopefully contain a return to the US.
